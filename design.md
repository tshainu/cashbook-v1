# Cash Book — Design Direction

## Brand
- App name: Cash Book
- Tagline: Simplified cash entry & Tracking app
- Logo style: "Cash" in cursive orange/red script, "BOOK" in bold dark red

## Colors
- Primary (teal-green): #419873
- Primary Dark: #2d7a5a
- Primary Light: #e8f5ef
- Accent Orange: #e07b2a
- Accent Red: #e03a2a
- Text Dark: #1a1a2e
- Text Medium: #555566
- Text Light: #888899
- Border: #e0e0e8
- White: #ffffff
- Background: #f8f9fa

## Typography
- Font: Poppins (headings bold, body regular)
- Display: 28px bold
- Heading: 20px semibold
- Body: 14px regular
- Caption: 12px

## Bottom Tab Bar
- Background: #419873 (teal)
- Icons: white, colored illustrations from design
- 3 tabs: Sales list (wallet icon) | New Sale (money bag icon) | Settings (gear icon)

## Components
- Cards: white bg, subtle shadow, rounded 12px
- Buttons: rounded 30px (pill shape), green for primary, red for danger
- Inputs: rounded border, icon prefix
- Numpad: large rounded square keys, red border stroke
- Modals: card overlay on blurred background
- Period tabs: teal active, white text; gray inactive

## Style
- White background, very clean
- Teal as dominant brand color
- Money/finance doodle pattern for splash screen
- Simple, fast, minimal — built for quick cash entry
